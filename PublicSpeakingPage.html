Module 1: Conquering Anxiety & Building Confidence

Overview
Learn why stage fright happens and how to turn fear into strength.
1.1 - The Science of Stage Fright
Understand glossophobia, fight-or-flight response, and physical symptoms.
1.2 - Mind/Body Prep Techniques
Use breathing, reframing, power poses, and physiological sigh to calm nerves.
1.3 - Audience & Environment Analysis
Overcome spotlight effect and prepare for different audiences and settings.
1.3 - Activity
Complete anxiety journal and create personal reframing statements.

Module 2: Crafting Your Message

2.1 - Defining Your Core Message
Identify the single most important idea your audience will remember.
2.1 - Quick Activity
Write your entire talk condensed into one powerful sentence.
2.2 - The Power of Story
Use personal, relatable stories to emotionally engage your listeners.
2.3 - The Essential Structure That Never Fails
Master the proven Hook → Problem → Solution/Insight → Call to Action flow.

Module 3: Mastering Delivery & Body Language

3.1 - Vocal Toolbox
Control pace, volume, pauses, tone variation, and eliminate filler words.
3.2 - Intentional Body Language
Use open posture, purposeful gestures, and confident movement on stage.
3.3 - Eye Contact and Connection
Apply the 3-second rule and scan pattern to build real audience connection.
Experiential Activity - Interactive Mirror & AI Review
Record your delivery and analyze voice, body, and presence with AI feedback.

Module 4: Presentation Design, Practice & Q&A

4.1 - Designing Effective Visuals
Create clean slides with minimal text, strong images, and good contrast.
4.2 - Rehearsal and Feedback
Do multiple timed rehearsals and get constructive feedback from others.
4.2 - Activity
Record a full practice run and review your performance critically.
4.3 - Handling The Unpredictable
Stay calm during tough questions, tech failures, or unexpected interruptions.

Module 5: Final Capstone Speech

5.1 - Final Speech + Mentor Feedback
Deliver your complete polished speech and receive detailed mentor review.
